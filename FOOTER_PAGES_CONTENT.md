# Footer Pages Content Plan

This document outlines industry-standard content to display for the footer links: **About**, **Privacy**, **Terms**, and **Contact**. The actual UI pages can be implemented later using this as the source.

> Note: This content is for educational use and is not legal advice.

---

## 1. About

**Purpose:** Briefly explain what SkillConnect is, who it serves, and why it exists.

**Sections to include:**

1. **Overview**
   - One–two sentences describing SkillConnect as a platform connecting customers with verified skilled workers and equipment suppliers in Sri Lanka.

2. **Mission & Values**
   - Short mission statement (e.g., making it easy and safe to hire trusted professionals).
   - 3–5 bullet values (trust, safety, transparency, fair opportunities for workers, etc.).

3. **How It Works (High-Level)**
   - 3 short bullets for each role:
     - Customers: browse workers and equipment, post jobs, leave reviews.
     - Workers: create a profile, manage availability, accept bookings.
     - Suppliers: list and manage equipment rentals.

4. **Safety & Quality**
   - Note on profile verification steps (ID checks, manual review, etc. – match what your app actually does).
   - Encourage users to review ratings and communicate through the platform.

5. **Our Story (Optional)**
   - 1 short paragraph on why the product was created (e.g., addressing fragmented informal labour market).


## 2. Privacy

**Purpose:** Summarize how user data is collected, used, and protected. For a real product this must be reviewed by a lawyer; keep it clear and honest.

**Sections to include:**

1. **Introduction**
   - Statement that this Privacy Policy explains how SkillConnect collects, uses, and protects personal information.
   - Mention the region (e.g., Sri Lanka) and applicable laws in generic terms.

2. **Information We Collect**
   - Account information (name, email, phone, password hash).
   - Profile details (location, bio, skill category, profile picture).
   - Booking and job information (jobs posted, bookings made, messages, reviews).
   - Technical data (device info, IP address, log data, cookies/analytics).

3. **How We Use Your Information**
   - To create and manage accounts.
   - To match customers with workers and suppliers.
   - To process bookings, payments (if applicable), and notifications.
   - To improve the service, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

4. **Sharing of Information**
   - With other users when necessary to provide the service (e.g., sharing limited profile info between customers and workers).
   - With service providers/partners (hosting, analytics, communications).
   - When required by law, to protect rights, or in connection with a business transaction.

5. **Data Retention**
   - High-level explanation of how long data is kept (e.g., while the account is active and for a reasonable period after).

6. **Your Choices & Rights**
   - Updating profile details.
   - Requesting account deletion.
   - Managing notifications and communication preferences.

7. **Security**
   - Statement that reasonable technical and organizational measures are used, but no system is 100% secure.

8. **Children’s Privacy**
   - Clarify that the service is not intended for children under a specific age (e.g., 18) and accounts must not be created for them.

9. **Changes to This Policy**
   - Mention that the policy may be updated and users will be notified of material changes.

10. **Contact Details**
   - Pointer to the Contact page/email for privacy-related questions.


## 3. Terms

**Purpose:** Define the rules for using the platform. For production use, this should be reviewed by legal counsel.

**Sections to include:**

1. **Acceptance of Terms**
   - Statement that by creating an account or using SkillConnect, users agree to these Terms.

2. **User Roles & Eligibility**
   - Age requirement and ability to enter into a contract.
   - Description of roles (customer, worker, supplier, admin).

3. **Accounts & Responsibilities**
   - Users must provide accurate information and keep credentials secure.
   - Responsibility for all activity under their account.

4. **Platform Role (Marketplace Disclaimer)**
   - Clarify that SkillConnect is a platform that connects parties and does not directly provide services or guarantee outcomes.
   - Workers and suppliers are independent, not employees or agents.

5. **Bookings, Jobs & Payments (High-Level)**
   - How bookings/jobs are requested and accepted.
   - High-level expectations for cancellation, punctuality, and behaviour.
   - If your project includes payments, include a short summary of payment handling and refunds (nothing binding/complex).

6. **User Conduct**
   - Prohibit illegal activities, harassment, discrimination, hate speech, fraud, and spam.
   - Require compliance with applicable laws and respect for others.

7. **Reviews & Ratings**
   - Rules for leaving fair and honest feedback.
   - Right to moderate or remove abusive or fake reviews.

8. **Content & Intellectual Property**
   - Ownership of the platform’s branding, UI, and content.
   - Limited license for users to access and use the service.
   - User-provided content (photos, descriptions) – user retains ownership but grants SkillConnect a license to display it.

9. **Termination & Suspension**
   - Rights to suspend or terminate accounts that violate policies.

10. **Disclaimers & Limitation of Liability**
   - Service provided “as is” without guarantees.
   - Liability caps in simple, non-legalistic language (for assignments keep this generic).

11. **Governing Law & Dispute Resolution**
   - Mention governing law (e.g., laws of Sri Lanka) and a simple dispute process (e.g., first contact support).

12. **Changes to the Terms**
   - Right to update the Terms and notice of material changes.


## 4. Contact

**Purpose:** Provide clear channels for users to reach the team.

**Sections to include:**

1. **Support Overview**
   - One–two sentences on when and why users should contact you (account issues, reporting problems, feedback, privacy requests).

2. **Contact Methods**
   - **Email:** e.g., `support@skillconnect.example` (replace with your project email).
   - **Phone:** placeholder support number (optional for assignment).
   - **Address:** generic office address (or indicate “Remote team based in Sri Lanka”).

3. **In-App Support**
   - Mention that users can also use the Messages or Help/Support section inside the app to reach the team.

4. **Response Times**
   - Simple expectation statement (e.g., “We aim to respond within 1–2 business days”).

---

## 5. Implementation Notes

- Each footer link (About, Privacy, Terms, Contact) should route to a dedicated page (e.g., `/about`, `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/contact`).
- For the assignment, keep content concise (1–3 short sections per page) but aligned with the structure above.
- If the app is only for academic use, you can clearly label the pages as **Sample** or **For academic demonstration only**.
